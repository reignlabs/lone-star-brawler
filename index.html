HOW TO PLAY

THE GOAL

You're THE LONE STAR — one stubborn fan in enemy colors. Clear the crowd in each round, survive 5 rounds, then go as deep as you can into endless OVERTIME. You have 3 lives. Build combos for big score.

MOVE

WASD or the ARROW keys to walk around the floor (up/down = depth).

Use the D-PAD on the left to walk around the floor.

FIGHT

  • J PUNCH — fast jab, low knockback.
  • K KICK — more reach and shove.
  • L HAYMAKER — slow heavy swing, huge knockback. Time it.
  • SPACE TURBO BUM RUSH — dash through the crowd, plowing fans aside. You can't be hit mid-dash.
  • PUNCH — fast jab, low knockback.
  • KICK — more reach and shove.
  • HMKR (Haymaker) — slow heavy swing, huge knockback.
  • RUSH (Turbo Bum Rush) — dash through the crowd. Can't be hit mid-dash.

THE CROWD

Hype fans rush you. Some just film on their phones and dodge. Throwers lob pizza from range. Big bruisers soak hits. On Round 5, a Bandwagon King shows up.

COPS

Officers wander in to keep the peace. They'll grab you — mash any attack button to break free — but they also tackle fans, so let them help.

POWER-UPS

Grab the floating boxes: Nachos heal · Foam Finger mega-knockback · Energy rage mode · Air Horn stuns the crowd · Lucky Jersey invincibility · Pretzel Nunchucks extra reach · Ref Whistle freezes everyone · Hot Dog Cannon projectiles.
